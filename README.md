## Key Insights

### Overview
- Generated $262.53M in total revenue with $166.93M in route profit, demonstrating strong profitability.
- The fleet covered 122M miles while maintaining an 83% utilization rate, indicating efficient asset usage.
- First Group was the top customer, while the Philadelphia–Seattle lane generated the highest revenue.

###  Driver Performance
- Drivers generated $257M in revenue from 84K completed trips.
- The top five drivers each generated over $4M in revenue, highlighting outstanding individual performance.
- The average revenue per driver was $1.72M, with an incident rate of 1.13 incidents per driver.

### Fleet & Maintenance
- Total maintenance expenditure reached $5.73M, averaging $0.05 per mile.
- Fleet utilization remained high at 83%, indicating effective fleet management.
- Inspection, repair, and preventive maintenance were the most common maintenance activities.
  
### Safety & Operations
- The operation completed 85K loads with only 170 incidents, resulting in a low 0.20% incident rate.
- 62.35% of incidents were non-preventable, while 37.65% were preventable, highlighting opportunities for targeted safety improvements.
- Refrigerated and Dry Van load types generated nearly equal revenue (about $131M each), demonstrating balanced operational performance.
